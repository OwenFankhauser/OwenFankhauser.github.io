A little more about me...

I graduated from Miami University May 17th, 2025 with my Bachelor's in Computer Science and a General Business Minor. During my time at Miami, I picked up a variety of different interests as I progressed through the curriculum. In particular, I enjoyed UI/UX development and answer-set programming. I was inspired by creating effective front-end tools that allowed users to intuitively accomplish their tasks while minimizing confusion and frustration. This ultimately led to an interest in game development in my Senior year. While attending Miami's Farmer School of Business, I quickly learned and applied Team Management and Team Building skills in Agile development settings to effiently lead or participate in group projects. When I wasn't studying, I acted as an independent contractor and worked in a local hardware store. Between these two positions, I learned to develop trade skills like locksmithing. This was a perfect environment to learn to network, develop customer service skills, and learn to work with different teams or individual clients.
